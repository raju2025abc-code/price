About Me

Hello! I'm Anik Hossan, a 22-year-old animator based in Manikganj, Bangladesh. With over 5 years of experience in 2D/3D animation, motion graphics, and visual storytelling, my journey began with a simple flipbook and grew into a professional career.

I thrive on challenges and am always exploring new techniques to push the boundaries of animation. My toolkit includes After Effects, Blender, Maya, and the Adobe Creative Suite.
